Handwritten Character Recognition using Convolutional Neural Network Technique

This paper presents a comprehensive study of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) applied to the field of character recognition. It elaborates on the development of a system capable of accurately identifying handwritten alphabets by training CNNs on various datasets, analyzing errors, optimizing learning techniques, and refining network parameters. The aim was to create a reliable, real-time character recognition tool for digital document automation and accessibility solutions.

Descriptive Analysis of Various Smart Road Architectures

This research explores the implementation of smart road infrastructures as part of intelligent transportation systems. It provides a thorough analysis of different architectural designs used to enable vehicle-to-infrastructure (V2I) communication, autonomous navigation, and smart traffic regulation. The study covers use cases, benefits, challenges, and comparative reviews of technological approaches adopted globally, with a vision toward safer, smarter roadways.

Era of E-Commerce: Crypto vs Stock

This paper investigates the shift in financial behavior in the e-commerce domain, with a focus on cryptocurrency and traditional stock investments. It draws comparisons on volatility, adoption rate, legal frameworks, user trust, and potential for disruption. Through analytical data and market sentiment analysis, the paper discusses how each financial instrument shapes the digital economy and influences the buying and investment preferences of users globally.
